# Subscription Plans

## Free Tier

The free tier includes 100 API calls per month, access to standard models, and community support. No credit card is required. Free accounts that have been inactive for 90 days may be deactivated.

## Pro Plan

The Pro plan costs $29 per month or $278 per year (20% discount). It includes 10,000 API calls per month, access to all standard and advanced models, email support with a 48-hour response time, and the ability to add up to 3 team members.

## Premium Plan

The Premium plan costs $99 per month or $950 per year. It includes unlimited API calls, access to all models including reasoning models, priority support with a 4-hour response time, custom rate limits, and up to 20 team members. Premium subscribers also receive early access to new features before general availability.

## Enterprise Plan

Enterprise plans are custom-priced based on usage volume and requirements. They include a dedicated account manager, SLA guarantees, SSO integration, custom data retention policies, and on-premise deployment options. Contact sales to discuss an enterprise agreement.

## Changing Plans

You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated for the remainder of the billing cycle. Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.

## Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing period. Your data is retained for 30 days after cancellation, after which it is permanently deleted.
