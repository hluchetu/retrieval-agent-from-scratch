# Billing Policy

## Payment Methods

We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and PayPal. All payments are processed securely through Stripe. Bank transfers are available for annual enterprise plans only.

## Billing Cycles

Monthly plans are billed on the same date each month. Annual plans are billed once per year at a discounted rate of 20% off the monthly equivalent. If your billing date falls on a day that does not exist in a given month (e.g. the 31st), you will be billed on the last day of that month.

## Failed Payments

If a payment fails, we retry the charge after 3 days, then again after 7 days. If the charge fails a third time, your account is downgraded to the free tier. You will receive an email notification at each stage. To reactivate your subscription, update your payment method and your plan will be reinstated immediately.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated automatically on each billing date and sent to the email address on your account. You can also download all past invoices from the Billing section of your account settings.

## Tax

Applicable taxes are calculated based on your billing address. Enterprise customers with a valid VAT number can submit it in account settings to have VAT removed from future invoices.
