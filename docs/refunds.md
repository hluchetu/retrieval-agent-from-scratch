# Refund Policy

## Standard Refunds

Refunds are not issued for monthly subscriptions once the billing cycle has started. If you cancel a monthly plan, your access continues until the end of the current billing period and no further charges are made.

## Annual Plan Refunds

Annual plans are eligible for a full refund within 14 days of the initial purchase, provided the account has made fewer than 5 API calls during that period. After 14 days, annual plans are non-refundable. If you cancel an annual plan after the refund window, your access continues until the end of the annual period.

## Premium Subscriber Refunds

Premium subscribers are not eligible for prorated refunds. When a premium subscription is cancelled, access continues through the end of the current billing period. Refunds for premium plans are only issued in cases of verified billing errors.

## Billing Errors

If you believe you were charged incorrectly, contact support within 7 days of the charge. Include your account ID and a description of the error. Verified billing errors are refunded within 5 business days to the original payment method.

## Exceptions

Refunds may be granted outside of these terms at our discretion in cases of extenuating circumstances. Contact support to discuss your situation.
